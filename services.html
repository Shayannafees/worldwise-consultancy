Our Services & Packages

Basic Package

  • Initial Consultation: Understand your academic background, career goals, and preferred destinations (USA, UK, China, Hungary, Tajikistan).
  • University Shortlisting: Based on your profile and aspirations, we'll help you identify suitable universities and programs.
  • Application Guidance (1-2 Universities): Step-by-step assistance with filling out application forms and preparing necessary documents for up to two universities.
  • Statement of Purpose/Letter of Recommendation Review: Feedback and editing support for your application essays.

Contact us for pricing

Standard Package

  • Everything in Basic Package, PLUS:
  • Comprehensive Application Support (3-5 Universities): Extended guidance for applications to 3 to 5 universities.
  • Visa Application Guidance: Detailed information and support for preparing your visa application documents and interview preparation.
  • Scholarship Information & Guidance: We'll inform you about potential scholarship opportunities and guide you through the application process.
  • Pre-Departure Briefing: Essential information on travel, accommodation, local culture, and settling in your chosen country.

Contact us for pricing

Premium Package

  • Everything in Standard Package, PLUS:
  • End-to-End Support (Unlimited Universities): Comprehensive assistance for all your university applications, no limit on the number.
  • Personalized Mentorship: Dedicated consultant providing ongoing support and guidance throughout your journey.
  • Interview Preparation: Mock interviews and personalized feedback to boost your confidence for university admissions and visa interviews.
  • Post-Arrival Assistance: Support with initial settling in, including airport pickup arrangements (if requested and feasible), accommodation advice, and local registration guidance.
  • Networking Opportunities: Introduction to alumni networks and student communities in your destination country.
